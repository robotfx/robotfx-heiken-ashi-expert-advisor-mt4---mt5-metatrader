<p>The <strong>RobotFX Heiken Ashi Expert Advisor (EA)</strong> is a complete <strong>trend trading solution</strong> for traders using MetaTrader MT4 or MT5. This Heiken Ashi EA can detect the trend—if enabled—and trade accordingly: it buys in an uptrend and sells in a downtrend based on the Heiken Ashi candle color on the selected timeframe. Trend detection can be disabled to allow trading purely on Heiken Ashi signals from the current timeframe, though following the trend is recommended for optimal results.</p>

<p>The Heiken Ashi EA allows full customization of candle types, moving average methods (smoothed, exponential, simple, linear), period, and shift for trend detection and trade entries. When trend detectors (A or B) are enabled, trades are only executed if the trend conditions are met, ensuring safer entries.</p>
<p>Trades are opened when Heiken Ashi candles change color (red to green for long trades, green to red for shorts) and can be validated with the swing zone feature to avoid trades against market structure. After opening trades, the EA automatically applies stop-loss, take-profit, and advanced trailing methods including Heiken Ashi, MA, PSAR, ATR, or other trailing algorithms. The EA can optionally use martingale if a previous trade closes in negative profit. This makes the Heiken Ashi EA an all-in-one solution for trend trading with <strong>automated trade management</strong>.</p>
